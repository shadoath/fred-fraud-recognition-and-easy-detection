Everything You Need to Stay Safe

Gmail Email Scanning

Open any email in Gmail and click Scan This Email. Fred reads the sender, subject, and body and tells you whether it's a scam. Premium users can enable auto-scan to check emails the moment they're opened.

Website Scanning

On any webpage, click Scan This Page. Fred analyzes the content for phishing patterns, impersonation, and fraud signals. Premium users can auto-scan pages every time they open the Fred popup.

Three-Tier Verdicts

Every scan returns one of three clear verdicts: Safe Zone, Be Careful, or Likely a Scam — along with a plain-English explanation and specific red flags found.

Scan History

Your last 20 scans are saved locally on your device. Each entry shows the type (Email or Website), verdict, and timestamp so you can quickly revisit past results without re-scanning.
