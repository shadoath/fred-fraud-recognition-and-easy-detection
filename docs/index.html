Gmail Email Scanning

Open any Gmail email and click Scan. Fred reads the email and tells you instantly if it looks like a scam. Enable auto-scan to check emails automatically as you open them.

Website Scanning

On any webpage, click Scan This Page. Fred analyzes the full page content for fraud signals, phishing patterns, and suspicious links.
