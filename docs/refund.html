14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you subscribe to FRED Premium and decide it's not for you, you can request a full refund within 14 days of your initial purchase. No questions asked, no hoops to jump through.

How to Request a Refund

Simply email us at info@fredsecurity.com with your license key or the email address you used to subscribe. We'll process your refund promptly.

Alternatively, you can request a refund directly through LemonSqueezy using the link in your purchase confirmation email.

After the 14-Day Window

After 14 days, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. If you're experiencing a technical issue that prevents FRED from working, we'll do our best to resolve it or offer a refund.

Cancellation

You can cancel your FRED Premium subscription at any time:

  • Use the cancellation link in any LemonSqueezy receipt email.
  • Or email us at info@fredsecurity.com and we'll cancel it for you.

When you cancel, your Premium access remains active until the end of your current billing period. You will not be charged again.

Free Tier

The free tier of FRED requires no payment and no refund applies. You can use it indefinitely or uninstall the extension at any time.

Contact

Questions about refunds? Email us at info@fredsecurity.com. We're real people and we respond promptly.
